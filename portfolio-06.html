Perfect Phone Repair Logo Design

Overview
Perfect Phone Repair shop is located at Rampai Business Park, KL Malaysia. On top of selling mobiles devices, they also provide repair services on mobile phone and tablets too. Click here to view this project!

Goal
The owner hopes to re-create the signage to highlight the repair services. Based on my study and observation, I inspired this idea from Doctor and Patient angle. I used "Stethoscope" to represent the meaning of "Repair" and "customer" as the patients.
